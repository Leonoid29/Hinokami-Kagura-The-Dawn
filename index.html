Hinokami Kagura

The Dawn

Summary

Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted boy living in the mountains with his family, wakes up one morning to take coal to sell in the nearby town, leaving his home under the watch of his mother and siblings, but when he returns after spending the night at a neighbor’s house, he finds his family brutally slaughtered by a demon, with only his sister Nezuko alive—but she’s been transformed into a demon, her body tiny and fragile yet strangely human; devastated and determined, Tanjiro sets off on a perilous journey to find a cure, meeting the stoic Water Hashira-in-training Urokodaki, who trains him rigorously in Water Breathing, honing his sword skills, speed, and stamina while Tanjiro struggles to control his compassion toward demons he faces; after passing the grueling Final Selection, he encounters his first real challenge on Mount Natagumo, battling the terrifying Spider Demon family alongside allies like the cowardly but lightning-fast Zenitsu and the wild, instinct-driven Inosuke, learning teamwork, strategy, and the horrifying truth about the Twelve Kizuki; from there, Tanjiro and his friends descend into the Entertainment District, facing Upper Moon Six; later the Swordsmith Village, the loss of Rengoku, and finally the Infinity Castle where Muzan is defeated and Nezuko regains her humanity.
